About

This is a weather app built to provide the current weather predictions and weather forcast for the following seven days.

Data Accuracy

accuracy of the app is highly dependant on the OpenWeather API. All the data is sourced from Open Weather Map. They come up with various techniques in weather predictions and also house a pool of weather data dating back to 1st January 1970. Unfortunately the data is taken from certain cities around the world which may not include your locality click here to read more about the data accuracy
